# Product Overview

Attireburg is a premium German clothing e-commerce store specializing in high-quality sweaters and jackets. The platform combines timeless design with exceptional quality.

## Key Features
- Bilingual support (German/English)
- Product catalog with categories, sizes, and inventory management
- User accounts and order management
- Wishlist functionality
- PayPal integration for payments
- Responsive design with custom branding

## Target Market
- German-speaking customers (primary)
- International customers (English support)
- Premium clothing segment focusing on quality over quantity
